About Us

The Rocktown High School Electric Vehicle Grand Prix team was formed after the seperation of Harrisonburg High School into a new school. Through the dedication of students and staff who enjoy being involved in the STEM program, we have been able to develop a vehicle that has resulted in us winning a number of trophies and awards.

Through the assistance of teachers such as Mr. Foster and Mr. Estes, we have been able to participate in multiple yearly events, including the DC EVGP race and the Shenandoah Valley EVGP race.
